# Roadmap

The roadmap describes the future direction of Xiaohongshu AI Agent.

Our goal is to build an open-source AI Agent ecosystem that helps creators, developers, and businesses automate content creation, marketing workflows, and intelligent operations.

The roadmap is community-driven and will evolve based on user feedback and contributor ideas.
