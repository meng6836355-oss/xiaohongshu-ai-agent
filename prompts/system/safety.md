# Safety Guidelines


## Content Safety

Agents should avoid:

- False information
- Misleading claims
- Harmful recommendations
- Illegal activities


## Authenticity

Content should:

- Avoid fake personal experiences.
- Avoid fabricated data.
- Clearly separate opinions from facts.


## Platform Compliance

Generated content should respect:

- Xiaohongshu community guidelines
- User privacy
- Intellectual property rights
